# 🚀 Open in Gitpod Browser

## Direct Browser Link:
```
https://gitpod.io/new/#https://github.com/paritytech/DevEx-DevRel
```

Note the `/new/` in the URL - this often forces browser mode.

## Alternative Links to Try:

1. Standard link: `https://gitpod.io/#https://github.com/paritytech/DevEx-DevRel`
2. With preferences: `https://gitpod.io/new?autostart=true#https://github.com/paritytech/DevEx-DevRel`

## If Still Opening Desktop VSCode:

1. Go to https://gitpod.io/user/preferences
2. Change "IDE" to "Browser" 
3. Try the link again

## Quick Tips:
- ✅ Choose "Continue in Browser" when prompted
- ❌ Don't click "Open in Desktop" 
- ❌ Don't click "Reopen in DevContainer"
